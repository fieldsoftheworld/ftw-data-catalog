# FTW Global — Prediction Confidence & Quality Layers (500 m)

![Global prediction confidence](https://data.source.coop/ftw/global-data/predictions/confidence/thumbnail.png)

Global 500 m raster layers that quantify the reliability of the [PRUE](https://github.com/fieldsoftheworld/ftw-baselines/releases) global agricultural field-boundary predictions ([Robinson et al. 2026](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.11055)), part of [Fields of the World](https://fieldsofthe.world). Derived from the full-resolution (10 m) model outputs on a common global grid (EPSG:4326, ~0.004167 deg/px, -180..180 lon, -60..84 lat; each 500 m cell ~ 50x50 of the 10 m pixels). Five products: a modeled confidence score; field & boundary prediction density (unfiltered and confidence-filtered); model entropy; an external cropland-consensus count; and precision/recall against that cropland consensus. These layers let users assess where the field predictions can be trusted and filter the [vector dataset](https://source.coop/ftw/global-data/predictions/vectors) accordingly. Temporal coverage inferred as the 2025 prediction year from the [paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.11055) (the cropland-consensus layer is year-independent); pending confirmation from the data authors.

## Processing Notes

500 m quality-indicator and confidence rasters computed from the full-resolution (10 m) PRUE model outputs on a common global grid (EPSG:4326, ~0.004167°/px, −180..180 lon, −60..84 lat; each 500 m cell ≈ 50×50 of the 10 m pixels). The modeled confidence layer is a Random Forest trained on model-internal indicators (entropy, prediction density) over the 24 FTW-labelled countries (leave-one-country-out mean AUC 0.842). Organised here as a STAC collection of five items, one per measurement; the two EPSG:3857 8-bit GeoTIFFs are web-display ("visual") assets.

## Known Issues

Temporal coverage is inferred as the 2025 prediction year from the paper (figures and the confidence-retention curve are 2025) and is pending confirmation from the data authors; the crop_count_mean (cropland-consensus) layer is year-independent external data. The exact threshold/method of the field-density "_filtered" variant is pending author confirmation (its values differ from the explicit conf0.4/conf0.5 products). The confidence layer is conservative in regions whose visual signature differs from FTW training data (e.g. smallholder systems outside the training distribution), where real fields may receive low confidence — use the unfiltered density and continuous confidence rather than the default 0.4 threshold there.
